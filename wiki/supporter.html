Supporter Plan

The Supporter plan is a optional subscription for Neocities. It costs 5$ a month and has the following benefits:

  • 50 GB of storage
  • The ability to add a domain to your site
  • Free SSL certificates for your domain
  • 3 TB of bandwidth
  • WebDAV support
  • The freedom to upload any filetype

The Trump Plan



The "Trump Plan" (also known as "The Fat Cat Plan") was first set up on the 27th of April 2017 as a rebuttal to the FCC's prepositional to scrap Net Neutrality.

If your IP address said you were in the US Senate, the FCC, the White House, or a Trump hotel you would have seen this page that informs the viewer of what the Trump Plan is all about.

It was implemented to oppose anti net neutrality lobbyists, and the plan is much more limited than a typical standard account. It has only 2 MB of storage and 604 MB of bandwidth. Additionally, any accounts with the plan have a unique Fat Cat profile badge.
