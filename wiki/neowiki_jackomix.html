Neowiki (jackomix)

Also see neowiki (minerobber).

Neowiki is the name of a Neocities wiki that was first started in November 2017 by jackomix, as a way to find "information about the web-host, Neocities".

So far the wiki has only one article; an article about Neocitie member, fauux.
