WebComics are comics that are published on the Web or on a mobile app. Some well-known WebComics are Cyanide & Happiness, Loading Artist, xkcd, and Extra Fabulous Comics.

The large majority of WebComics on Neocities are self-published, that is to say that there is no large involvement of any kind from an established publisher. A number of people have published their comics on Neocities, all adding their own style of writing, humour, and art style.
