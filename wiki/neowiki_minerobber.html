neowiki (minerobber)

Also see Neowiki (jackomix).

neowiki is a wiki that was first made pubic in early September 2018 by minerobber, the wiki itself follows that of Neocities, and the community surrounding it.

The wiki is in part maintained on GitHub, where people can edit articles. Some of the articles as of September 2018 are; Neocities CLI, Supporter Plan, and Site Profile.

The neowiki has some similarities to Penny's Pages, for example, both of the wikis were made in the later half of 2018, both of the wikis use an open license of some sort (Creative Commons for Penny's Pages, and a MIT License for neowiki), and both have a lot of things in common on the style guide.
