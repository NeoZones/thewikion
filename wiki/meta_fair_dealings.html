META: Fair Dealings

Simply put, any media (be it text, images, audio, or anything else) not made by anyone working for, or with, Penny's Pages may be used on Penny's Pages, as the use of the media is used to help review a subject. This is what is know as "Fair Use", and it is an exceptions to copyright.

Because you're probably on the Web right now, you can look up Fair Dealing if you are unsure about what it is.
