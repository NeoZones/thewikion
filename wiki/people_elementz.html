elementz (no caps) is an American Neocities user who joined in October 2017.

His Neocities site hosts facts about elements of the periodic table, facts about planets of the solar system, temperature converter, and more!

elementz' first month of his site creation started with a complete project of Elementz v1. He originally made this project as his personal one, but he was so proud of the outcome. He wanted to show the project to another discord server called Developer Hangout. Now, his site is very successful, and has over 43K views.
