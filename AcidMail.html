AcidMail

AcidMail is a weekly newsletter written and published on the NeoZones website that comes out every Tuesday, the newsletter itself is written in ASCII and does not have any images attached to it, it covers a wide range of subjects. Recurring parts in the newsletter is the "CHECK THIS SHIT OUT!" part, where usually ten links are linked, the newsletter also has "ASCII OF THE NOW", a "weekly thing we do where we dig threw the old archives of forgotten websites, finding ASCII art that time forgot".
