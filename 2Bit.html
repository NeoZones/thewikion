2Bit

2Bit is the name of a 1990's themed website notable for its use of a literal 2-bit graphics (meaning it uses four colors, rather than a greater amount, as seen with other websites), created in late 2015.

The contents for the site includes a collection of over 100 Game Boy Camera photos, off the site in 2016, 2Bit was featured in "GeoCities Sites: The Internet in the 90s", an article written by Kuro Pixel where they write that "The person that made [2Bit] is really dedicated to [the theme of having everything in 2-bit color], because all graphics follow the rules of being 2 bit. Not a lot of real content on the site, but it's pretty unique so I thought I'd mention it".

The owner of 2Bit was also the creator of the now defunct NeoForums, that shut down sometime around 2017-12.
