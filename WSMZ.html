Website Profile

The Wicked Sick Meme Zone is Baguette's personal website, offering content including software, lazy music "reviews", and a chatroom. The website itself is known to have a number of W3C validity violation, with tags in incorrect places.

Bannerlink

WSMZ offers a free-to-use banner ad service, Bannerlink, offering anybody the oppurtunity to advertise themselves as long as they meet the "don't be a dick" quota. There are currently 22 ads running on the service, 3 of which being for Baguette's own pages.

Bannerlink is free to add to your site, with the only requirement being to add the following iframe: 

<iframe width="468" height="60" style="border:none" src="https://hbaguette.neocities.org/bannerlink/embed.html" name="bannerlink"></iframe>

Webrings

WSMZ is a member of 2 webrings, the [[Hotline Webring]] and the Ringlink webring, a webring that is also hosted on the site.

Ringlink operates by having wanting-members E-Mailing their own individual control panel, with their buttons linking to a page on WSMZ which then redirects to the target site. In order to join, users are encouraged to contact Baguette.

JAUP

WSMZ was JAUP'd some time in 2019-06, with the description: "a music list, software downloads, and a bunch of other stuff in crusty web 1.0!"
